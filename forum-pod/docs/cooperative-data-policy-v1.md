# Cooperative Data Policy v1 (Formation Pilot)

**Version:** `coop-data-policy/2026-05-01`

## Purpose

The Forum Data Cooperative Association acts as a **non-custodial fiduciary** for opted-in civic feedback used to produce aggregate community intelligence reports.

## Your data

- You retain ownership of data in your **personal Pod**.
- The Association does not claim title to your raw contributions.
- Only submissions you **opt in** are copied to cooperative systems for aggregation.

## Opt-in

- Toggle “Share with cooperative” in Settings before submitting, or per submission when enabled.
- Opt-in exports are signed from your device and include your WebID for audit.

## After aggregation

- Raw opted-in copies are **deleted** from cooperative servers after confirmed aggregation.
- You receive notice in your Pod when wipe completes (pilot: notification resource).

## Public reports

- Reports include **opt-in counts only** — not a census of all residents.
- A **7-day member review period** runs before public listing when status is `review`.
